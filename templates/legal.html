BEST PRACTICES

Legal Guidelines.

STEP 01
📸
Preserve Evidence
Capture screenshots immediately. Do not crop or edit them. Ensure usernames, timestamps, and URLs are clearly visible.
STEP 02
🛡️
Do Not Engage
Avoid responding to the abuser. Retaliation can complicate the legal standing of your evidence. Block and document instead.
STEP 03
📑
Establish Patterns
Record repeated threats or harassment over time. A documented pattern of behaviour is critical for legal proceedings.
STEP 04
🚩
Platform Reporting
Report abuse to the platform where it occurred (Instagram, X, etc.). Record your report ticket numbers as supplementary evidence.
STEP 05
🚔
Law Enforcement
If physical threats, extortion, or blackmail are involved, contact local authorities or cybercrime divisions immediately.
STEP 06
📄
Generate PDF Reports
Use EvidenceVault to generate cryptographically signed, time-stamped PDF reports to present as organised legal documentation.
